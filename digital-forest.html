Digital Forest

An interactive, accessible website for a fictional sustainable tech company called Digital Forest. The goal was to create a clean, modern digital environment, that explores Digital Experiences and that reflects values like sustainability, accessibility, innovation, and user-centered design.

Role

Web Design / User Experience

Category

Digital Experiences

Artifacts

03

Status

Selected Work

Challenge

Design a visually engaging and fully accessible website for a fictional sustainability-focused tech brand. The goal was to communicate complex topics like renewable infrastructure and digital equity in a way that was modern, inclusive, and user-friendly.

Solution

Applied atomic design principles to break the interface into accessible components, used a bento-style layout for visual clarity, and followed WCAG guidelines for color contrast, text hierarchy, and keyboard navigation.

Outcome

A visually engaging and fully accessible website that effectively communicates the brand's values and mission while providing a seamless user experience.
