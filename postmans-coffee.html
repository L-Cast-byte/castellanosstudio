Postman's Coffee

Brand identity system exploring packaging, product hierarchy, and visual storytelling across multiple touchpoints.

Role

Brand Identity / Packaging / Visual System

Category

Brand Identity Systems

Artifacts

10

Status

Selected Work

Challenge

Create a flexible brand system that could organize multiple products while still feeling warm, recognizable, and easy to navigate.

Solution

The identity uses a courier-inspired visual language, structured packaging hierarchy, and repeatable graphic elements to connect each product within one cohesive system.

Outcome

A scalable packaging and identity system that supports product variety while maintaining a clear and memorable brand voice.
